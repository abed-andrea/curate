# Curate

A distraction-free space to save, organize, and actually act on content from social media — without having to reopen the apps that make you scroll.

### The Problem: 
Social media algorithms are great at surfacing content worth keeping — recipes, books, travel ideas, career tips. But the save features are buried inside the same apps designed to keep you scrolling. Saved content piles up, stays unorganized, and never gets acted on.


### The Solution:
Curate pulls that content out into a dedicated, distraction-free space. Save anything by pasting a link or uploading a screenshot, organize it into your own categories, and track what you have and haven't acted on — so things you save actually turn into things you do.
